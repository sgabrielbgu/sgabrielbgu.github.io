This is one of my longest-running research threads: designing and analyzing algorithms for network caching, including LPM caching for forwarding tables with splicing ({% cite gozlan2023goto %}), indicator-based caching with bandwidth constraints and false-negative awareness ({% cite cohen2022falsenegative %}, {% cite cohen2021power %}, {% cite cohen2021selfadjusting %}), and access strategies for caching under imprecise knowledge ({% cite cohen2021access %}, {% cite cohen2019access %}, {% cite keren2023latency %}). Much of this work provides worst-case (competitive ratio) performance guarantees alongside practical evaluation.
